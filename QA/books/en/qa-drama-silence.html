Title page

When the Silence Returns

Can Mara expose the lie that killed her brother if the truth also proves her family survived on the same silence?

Mediator Mara Iliev returns to the flood-struck town of Cernav after her brother dies in what officials call an accident. With archivist Iona Radu she uncovers a compensation fraud hidden inside the mediation office, but exposing it would strip Mara's mother of the pension that kept the family alive.
