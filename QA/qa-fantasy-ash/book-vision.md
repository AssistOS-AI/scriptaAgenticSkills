# Book vision

## Core idea

- Title: The Ash Keeps Your Name
- Book ID: qa-fantasy-ash
- Baseline profile: fantasy
- Work form: novelette
- Brief: An oath-breaker heir returns to a cursed valley and must expose the vow that gave her family power.

## Short story

Exiled heir Neris Vale returns to the ash valley of Drovna when the oath-fires begin whispering forbidden names. Guided by Brother Caelan Vey, she learns that House Vale protected the valley by burning witness memory into a vow furnace that turned obedience into law. To save the valley honestly, Neris must break the family oath in public and persuade the valley to accept a new protection built on remembered names instead of erased ones.

## Author-specified characters

- Neris Vale - oath-breaker heir; wants to sever the family vow before the valley binds another successor; needs to accept that belonging now requires relinquishment, not inheritance; fears becoming the next keeper of the same coercive system.
- Brother Caelan Vey - disgraced oath-keeper; wants to guide Neris to the hidden chronicles; needs to stop mistaking custodianship for innocence; fears watching Neris repeat the old compromise.
- Regent Maeron Vale - temporary ruler and pressure figure; wants to bind a new heir before winter spirits test the wards; fears a public naming of the children consumed by the rite.

## Author-specified scenes

- Ash speaks the forbidden name - the fire whispers Neris's dead sister's name on the terrace.
- Chronicle beneath the chains - the catacomb records prove that memory was part of the sacrifice.
- Ward breach at the mountain gate - truth affects the ward more strongly than ritual obedience does.
- The furnace broken in public - Neris names the dead during the valley rite and breaks the binding seal.

## Author-specified locations

- The ash terrace above Drovna village - cedar smoke, warm soot under snowlight, bells muffled by volcanic dust; home and surveillance occupy the same breath.
- The vow furnace catacombs - ember breath, iron prayer chains, old ash beneath wax; the dynasty's hidden engine where legitimacy is burned into permanence.

## Author constraints

- The valley wards hold only while an heir keeps the furnace vow active through lineage-bound ritual obedience.
- Each renewal burns witness memory from one household chosen by the ruling house.
- A ward can be reforged through named communal consent only after the hidden dead are spoken publicly.
- Stakes: the valley's survival, the memory of the consumed dead, and Neris's right to belong without domination.
