# The Ash Keeps Your Name

Exiled heir Neris Vale returns to the ash valley of Drovna when the family oath-fires begin speaking the names of the dead aloud. Guided by oath-keeper Brother Caelan, she learns that her dynasty's power comes from a vow furnace that burns witness memories into political obedience, and breaking it will strip the valley of the same protection that kept the winter spirits out.
