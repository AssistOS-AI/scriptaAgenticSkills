# The River Knows First

## Core idea
A police case turns inside out when Nadia Voss learns that procedure itself has become the cover-up.

## Short story
Inspector Nadia Voss reopens a river drowning that the county police ruled accidental. Tide logs, surveillance gaps, and witness transfers lead back to a redevelopment contract and a precinct-controlled evidence trail. Diver-journalist Theo Mercer helps her chase the physical proof while precinct chief Soren Arden weaponizes procedure against the case. Nadia can keep obeying the chain of command or break it before the institution washes the murder clean.

## Story question
Can Nadia prove murder before procedure itself is used to erase the evidence?

## Dilemma
Obey the chain of command and lose the victim forever, or break protocol and prove the station protected the killing.

## Main characters
- **Nadia Voss** - homicide inspector; outward goal: prove the victim was killed before he entered the river lock; hidden need: accept that lawful procedure can become the crime's most reliable accomplice.
- **Theo Mercer** - river diver turned investigations reporter; outward goal: recover the missing lock-camera drive; hidden need: stop mistaking cynicism for immunity.
- **Soren Arden** - precinct chief; outward goal: close the file before the redevelopment deal is signed; hidden need: believe that preserving departmental stability still counts as public service.

## Main locations
- **The river evidence hangar beside Lock Six** - diesel sheen, wet rope, fluorescent glare; a room where facts are tagged just long enough to be reassigned.
- **The abandoned sluice control room above the spillway** - rust, drowned electrical hum, hidden mechanisms; the place where sabotage becomes material.

## Key scenes
1. **Autopsy against the report** - Nadia spots blunt-force trauma hidden beneath the water damage.
2. **Camera gap at Lock Six** - Nadia and Theo connect the missing footage to a signed maintenance override.
3. **Diversion beneath the spillway** - they recover the drive case and proof that the precinct itself is obstructing the case.
4. **Briefing room breach** - Nadia projects the footage live before Soren can bury it again.

## Constraints
- Institution: **County Precinct South**
- Plot object: **the missing lock-camera drive**
- Motif: **watermarked evidence tags**
- World rule: any river death inside the redevelopment corridor is reviewed by the precinct before the civil prosecutor.
- Cost: procedural errors let the redevelopment board void testimony and delay charges indefinitely.
- Hard pressure: Nadia cannot move the evidence outside the precinct without committing the very breach Soren is waiting to punish.
