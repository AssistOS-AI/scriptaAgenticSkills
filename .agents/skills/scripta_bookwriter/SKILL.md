# SCRIPTA BookWriter

This skill creates the final book editions for a workspace. It preserves append-only export history and keeps the export stage downstream from planning and drafting while emitting single-file HTML reader editions, embedded cover art, and bilingual EN/RO outputs. During the QA campaign, the latest readable editions are also published to `QA/books/<lang>/`.
